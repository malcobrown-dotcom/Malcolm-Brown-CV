## Senior Technology Business Management, PMO, Workforce \& Transformation Leader

I am a senior business management, PMO, workforce and transformation leader with more than 25 years of experience across financial services, insurance and global technology organisations.

At the heart of my work is practical problem-solving. I am most effective when information is fragmented, a process is failing, accountability is unclear, or an organisation knows that something must change but does not yet have a workable route forward. I establish what is actually happening, close the data and control gaps, build an initial solution, prove that it works, and then standardise, automate or scale it.

My approach combines analysis, curiosity, persistence, technology and relationships. I bring together incomplete data, operational knowledge and different stakeholder perspectives to create a reliable view of the problem. Where necessary, I build a manual solution first to restore control or generate insight, then improve the process through better data, reporting, automation, governance or AI. I also identify practices that work elsewhere, adapt them to the new context and build the consensus needed to make them sustainable.

My work sits at the intersection of strategy, operations, people, finance, data and technology. I have supported COO, CIO and CTOO leadership; built and led PMO and business-management capabilities; governed large technology budgets and portfolios; designed enterprise workforce, cost and vendor models; created performance and organisational-health frameworks; and led culture, learning and employee-engagement programmes across complex multinational organisations.

Selected outcomes include more than **USD 30M in technology efficiencies delivered within eight months**, the first accurate workforce and future-state planning view across approximately **3,800 technology employees and 14+ locations**, governance of a **USD 60M+ project portfolio**, management of a **USD 40M annual technology budget and USD 80M application cost base**, and KPI and organisational-health frameworks supporting organisations of up to **4,000 employees**.

I bring a strong human dimension to transformation. I use co-creation, clear communication, coaching and practical stakeholder engagement to bring people with me rather than impose change on them. I have designed fairer talent and succession processes, built high-performing regional teams, created direct dialogue between employees and senior leaders, and developed learning, mentoring and capability programmes that connect organisational strategy with individual opportunity.

I also build practical AI-enabled applications, agents, specialist personas, prompt frameworks and multi-stage workflows. I am a business-led solution builder rather than a traditional software engineer: I translate needs into structured requirements, workflows, controls and test cases, then use AI-assisted prototyping and iteration to create tools that reduce repetitive work, improve analysis and strengthen decision-making.

## How I Create Value

My career has followed a consistent pattern:

1. **Find the real problem** — test assumptions, listen to the people doing the work, trace the data and identify the root cause rather than treating symptoms.
2. **Create visibility** — close information gaps, reconcile inconsistent sources and build a reliable view of cost, workforce, risk, delivery or organisational performance.
3. **Stabilise the operation** — create practical manual controls, reporting, governance or processes so leaders can make decisions and teams can continue to deliver.
4. **Simplify and improve** — remove unnecessary steps, clarify ownership, standardise definitions and adapt proven methods from elsewhere.
5. **Automate and scale** — use technology, reporting tools, workflow design and AI to reduce effort, improve accuracy and make the solution sustainable.
6. **Bring people with me** — co-create objectives, explain the rationale, build cross-functional consensus and give people the tools and confidence to adopt the change.
7. **Track the outcome** — define measures, maintain accountability and follow actions through to completion rather than allowing commitments to disappear after approval.
